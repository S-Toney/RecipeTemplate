Chocolate Cherry Cookies

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

Brief keyword packed introduction that will help w/SEO but not piss off someone wanting a quick answer to how to make some scrumptious Chocolate Cherry Cookies.

  • Prep Time: 15 min
  • Oven Temp: 350°
  • Bake Time: 8-10 min
  • Yield: 2.5 dozen
  • Allergies: eggs

Ingredients

  • 1C (2 sticks) butter softened
  • 1C sugar
  • 1C brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2t vanilla extract
  • 3C all purpose flour
  • 3/4C cocoa powder
  • 1t baking soda
  • 1/4t salt
  • 1C dried cherries

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°
  2. Mix dry ingredients in mid size bowl: sift the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, & salt.
  3. Mix wet ingredients in a small bowl: crean the butter, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract.
  4. Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until mixed and add the cherries.
  5. Bake on a nonstick or pre-prepared baking sheet for 8-10 minutes. Allow cookies to cool for a few minutes before transfurring to a cooling rack
